Hololive Production (ホロライブプロダクション) or simply known as hololive(ホロライブ), is an agency of Virtual YouTubers created by Cover Corp.

As of November 2019, the total amount of subscribers for the VTubers on YouTube and bilibili are 2.6 million and 3.5 million respectively with four of the VTubers coming in on the top 10 spot for VTubers on bilili. They are Shirakami Fubuki (2nd), Minato Aqua (5th), Natsuiro Matsuri (7th), and Akai Haato (9th).

On 13 June 2019, they announced the start of the audition for the third batch of hololive VTubers called Hololive Fantasy (ホロライブファンタジー). Application started from 13 June until 23 June with the screening starting and ending a day later and the interviews from 14 June until 28 June. During hololive karaoke party on 7 July, two of the members, Usada Pekora and Uruha Rushia, were announced.

By December 25, the 4th batch was announced.

Suisei is a modest, multitalented singer and aspiring idol who is among the more level-headed members of hololive. However, after her unexpectedly compelling performance as a traitor in a game of Project Winter, she gained a reputation for remorseless sociopathy behind a facade of polite sweetness. No one seems to know how much of the "Psychopath Suisei" persona is real or not, but Suisei herself acknowledges the comedic potential of the joke and has referred to the incident on multiple occasions.

Tokoyami Towa (常闇トワ) is a female Japanese Virtual YouTuber endorsed by hololive. She is part of hololive's fourth generation of VTubers alongside Tsunomaki Watame, Kiryu Coco, Amane Kanata and Himemori Luna.

Her Twitter account was created on November 2019, she made her first tweet on 31 December, her YouTube channel was created on 4 December and her debut stream was on 3 January 2020.
